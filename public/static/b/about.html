Movement patterns

Integrated in the design are movement sensors, allowing us to have an understanding as to how people behave around the structure, which places are more inviting and are potential bottle necks. Information regarding people movement allows us to better understand the strengths and weaknesses and react to them, having a structure which is responsive to its surroundings.

Modcam sensors were used, collecting anonymous data and transmitting it using wifi to remote servers. That data is later used as the base for visualizations and animations, showing movement patterns during a time frame of accumulative movement intensity
